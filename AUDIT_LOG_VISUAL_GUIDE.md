## ğŸ” Entity-Specific vs Global Logs

### Global Logs (Most Pages)
Shows **all** activities across the system:
- Dashboard
- Projects list
- Contractors list
- Payments list
- Settings

### Entity-Specific Logs
Shows activities for **one specific entity**:
- Documents page (filtered to current project)
- Could be used in Project Detail view
- Could be used in Contractor Detail view
