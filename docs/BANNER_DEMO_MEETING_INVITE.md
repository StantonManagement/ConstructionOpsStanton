## Questions to Answer

Come to the meeting ready to discuss:

1. **How does Banner handle the consolidated dashboard?**
   - This is Dan's #1 feature request (Task 5)
   - Does Banner solve this better than our current approach?

2. **What's their onboarding experience like?**
   - How fast can new users get productive?
   - What can we learn for our own onboarding?

3. **How do they handle mobile/field workflows?**
   - Daily logs, photos, check-ins
   - Is it truly mobile-first?

4. **What's their contractor experience?**
   - Do contractors have portal access?
   - How do they submit bids, payments, daily logs?

5. **What integrations do they offer?**
   - QuickBooks (our Task 10)
   - Other tools Dan uses

6. **What are users complaining about in reviews?**
   - What gaps can we exploit?
   - What mistakes should we avoid?

## Note to Dan

This competitive analysis will help us:

- **Validate our roadmap** - Are we building the right features?
- **Identify gaps** - What are we missing that users expect?
- **Avoid mistakes** - Learn from their UX problems
- **Differentiate** - Find opportunities to be better
- **Set realistic expectations** - Understand market standards

The goal is NOT to copy Banner, but to ensure Construction Ops is competitive and meets modern user expectations for construction management software.
