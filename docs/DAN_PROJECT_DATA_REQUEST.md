## Information Needed for Each Project

### Basic Information
- **Project Name**
- **Property Address** (street, city, state, zip)
- **Project Type** (renovation, new construction, repair, emergency, etc.)
- **Client/Owner Name**
- **Client Contact** (phone and email)

### Budget & Timeline
- **Total Budget** (overall project cost)
- **Amount Spent to Date**
- **Start Date**
- **Target Completion Date**
- **Current Phase** (planning, permits, foundation, framing, rough-in, finish, punch list, etc.)
- **Estimated % Complete**

### Team & Contractors
For each contractor working on the project:
- **Company Name**
- **Trade** (plumbing, electrical, framing, excavation, etc.)
- **Contact Person**
- **Phone Number**
- **Email**
- **Budget Allocated** (how much of the total budget is for this contractor)

### Current Status
- **What's happening now?** (brief description)
- **Any blockers or urgent items?**
- **What needs immediate attention?**
- **Who are you waiting on?** (inspector, contractor, client decision, permits, etc.)
- **When do you need to follow up?**
